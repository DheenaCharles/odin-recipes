Pizza

Here is how to make a yummy pizza!

Ingredians needed

  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Cheese
  • Pizza sause
  • Vegetables for topping

Steps

  1. Take a all purpose four and salt in a bowl and mix it well.
  2. Add east, sugar and water to it.
  3. Prepare a doe with the above mixture and leave it for 15 minutes.
  4. Take a piece of doe and roll it to required size.
  5. Apply pizza sause on it.
  6. Sprinkle some cheese on it.
  7. Add a layer of your favourite Vegetables or sausage or any topping of your choice.
  8. Keep this in oven for 25 minutes.
  9. Enjoy your pizza.
