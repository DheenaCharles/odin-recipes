Momos

Here is how to make a yummy Momos!

Ingredians needed

  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Spinach
  • chillies

Steps

  1. Take a all purpose four and salt in a bowl and make a doe.
  2. Leave the doe for 15 minutes.
  3. Take a piece of doe and roll it into small flat circles.
  4. Now keep a pan on the flame and add oil to it.
  5. Add green chillies and some spices to it.
  6. Add the spinach.
  7. Add salt to taste and cook the mixture for 10 minutes.
  8. Let the cooked mixture cool then stuff it into the rolled doe.
  9. Boil the dumplings in water for 10 minutes and its ready to serve.
  10. Enjoy your momos.
