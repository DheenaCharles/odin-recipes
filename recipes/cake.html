Cake

Here is how to make a yummy cake!

Ingredians needed

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Coco powder

Steps

  1. Take a bowl and put all purpose flour in it.
  2. Add Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Baking powder and Coco powder to it.
  3. Mix it well.
  4. Then add some butter and again mix it well.
  5. Apply butter on a tray.
  6. Pour out the mixture to it.
  7. Keep it in the oven for 25 minutes.
  8. Enjoy the cake.
